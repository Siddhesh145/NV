Business Name

Starting a business or venture is an emotional decision as it has hopes, aspiration, ambition, hard work, commitments and investments of the creator. Also, to succeed, it needs the right support from employees and the trust of its customers.

The first and the foremost part of starting a venture, is to give a name to your business or brand. Not only is it an identity that you and your employees will emotionally connect with, but also is the first thing that your customers will learn and through which make an impression about you and your product/service.

Fortunately for us, the power of vibration of Characters, Sound and Numbers don’t restrict themselves to just humans, their personalities & their destinies. Their influences are equally felt to our material creations as well.

Business Names, Addresses, Phone Numbers and Colour of Logo’s show remarkable affinity to Alphabets, Words, Sound and Numbers. Hence, they hold tremendous power and can play an important role in the way your business is perceived, accepted, grows and attracts financial success.

Many a times we see great ideas, vision, hard work and investments not resulting into the desired success. On deeper analysis, we can see that it is the numbers supporting the business that don’t resonate the vibrations of success.

The Name of your business specifically play an important role in defining the following finer aspect:

  1. Weather it correctly depicts the nature of your business i.e. Technical, Creative, Mechanical, Philanthropy, Inspirational etc.
  2. How your customers will perceive your identity and weather it is destined or designed to attract financial success.

Hence, coupled with the right intent, ethics and effort made by the creators of the business, a name incorporating such numbers is likely to attract the desired business success.
